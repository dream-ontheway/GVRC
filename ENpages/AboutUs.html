About Us

Introduction

China（Shanghai）Free Trade Zone Jinqiao Global Virtual Reality Conference (referred as GVRC following) will be a multi-functional series of brand activities which is long-term, national grade and international. Through the one-stop service system of both online and offline combing the display, conference, park and investment, it provides the foundation and value-added services to VR and AR industry, and promotes the cooperation of different industries and builds up the high-class industry groups, and look for mutual-benefit partners for jointing, and also provides all kinds of services such as forum, exhibition, discussion and investigation to those benefit-relating groups. At the same time, it can also gather strength from all sides to make China（Shanghai）Free Trade Zone Jinqiao area become an international virtual reality industrial park, and optimize the VR and AR industrial structure, thus promotes the industrial upgrading.

GVRC is a grand meeting that faced up with all the pioneers and practitioners around the world in virtual reality industry. With the topic of “New World Innovation”, the first GVRC conference willbe held in Jinqiao Shanghai on June 8-9, 2016. This conference will mainly proceed in mode of forum and display.
