The First Global Virtual Reality Conference – VAR is about to sail in China!
The First Global Virtual Reality Conference VAR is about to sail in China!

How popular VR is right now? The industry of VR has successfully received multiple billon dollars’ investments involved with those tech giants like Facebook, Google, Sony, Valve. It is estimated that there are more than 10 VR events in China in the last two months. The one that you should never miss must be the First Global Virtual Reality Conference.

It will be a grand meetingfor VR industry to be a most cutting-edge technology, to be new experience to overturn tradition and to be applied in various areas.  The grant VR party will open soon!

Under the guidance of Economy and Information Technology Commission of Shanghai Municipality, the First Global Virtual Reality Conference will be host on June 8th and 9th, co-hosted by Economy and Information Technology Commission of Shanghai Pudong New Area, Jinqiao Bureau of China (Shanghai) Pilot Free Trade Zone Administration, Shanghai Jinqiao Economic and Technological Development Administration Committee, China Europe International Business School E-platform, Shanghai Jinqiao Export Processing Zone Development Co., Ltd., and Migu Video Technology Co., Ltd.

The first GVRC with the topic of “New World innovation” will gather world famous elites in VR industry. We invited professionals and pioneers from VR movies to VR games, and also from VR socialization to VR medicine, together with many famous investing agencies and media.

The most influential senior managers, innovators and industry pioneers in global VR Industry will gather in GVRC, learn the trend and application of VR technology, and work together to promote the development and integration of VR industry. GVRC will work on building and promoting the platform for resource integration and promotion of global VR industry. Let’s expect the sailing of Chinese VR industry together.
