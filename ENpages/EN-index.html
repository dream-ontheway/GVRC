A gathering of VR/AR global leaders

The first GVRC with the topic of “New World innovation” will be host at Jinqiao China Europe International Business college on June 8th and 9th. The two-day conference includes forums with themes of technology, industrial applications and investment, top startups, and a exhibition of current technology and products in the world. Aim at promoting the effective integration of virtual reality and traditional industries, and achieve industrial innovation.

Summit for representative VR and AR enterprises and innovation-oriented ventures of various industries

Leaders from various elite industries; domestic giants involved in the industry; outstanding domestic start-ups; the representatives of investing companies which are interested in this industry; the innovative companies covering medical, education, travel, home, Ecommerse , real estate , entertainment and other industrial applications; the competent department heads of relevant government sectors and media attend the forum.

Global Virtual reality conference

Under the guidance of Economy and Information Technology Commission of Shanghai Municipality, the conference is co-hosted Economy and Information Technology Commission of Shanghai Pudong New Area, Jinqiao Bureau of China (Shanghai) Pilot Free Trade Zone Administration, Shanghai Jinqiao Economic and Technological Development Administration Committee, China Europe International Business School E-platform, Shanghai Jinqiao Export Processing Zone Development Co., Ltd., and Migu Video Technology Co., Ltd.
